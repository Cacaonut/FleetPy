Every file has the format:

A) For deterministic demand
detDemand_{duration of PUDO}_{demand size}.csv

duration of PUDO: in seconds
demand size: number of trips


B) For stochastic duration:
stoDemand_{distribution type in PascalCase}_{parameter 1 distribution}_{parameter 2 distribution}_{demand size}.csv

distribution type in PascalCase: either LogLog (log logarithmic distribution) or LogNorm (log normal distribution)
parameter 1 distribution: alpha (scale parameter of LogLog) or mu (mean parameter of LogNorm) [in seconds]
parameter 2 distribution: beta (shape parameter of LogLog) or sigma (st. dev. of LogNorm) [in seconds]
demand size: number of trips
